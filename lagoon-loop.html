Lagoon Loop

A maker–checker quant loop you can watch overfit in real time. Same architecture the “prints alpha while you sleep” post sells — instrumented so the mirage is visible.

Paper / simulation only. No broker, no real money. The point isn’t to find alpha — it’s to watch the verifier wave through luck and see in-sample Sharpe evaporate once you account for trials, costs, and out-of-sample data.

Why this is a simulator, not the daemon in the post

A browser tab can’t run on a schedule unattended, and it deliberately never touches a broker. The autonomous “while you sleep” version is Stage 2: a Python service on your own always-on machine, wired to a paper-trading API. This stage exists to teach you why that daemon won’t print money before you give it any.
